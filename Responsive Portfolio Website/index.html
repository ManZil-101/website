About me

Subhash Sharma

A Physics Graduate | Research Interest in High Energy Physics | Freelancer | Editor

I'm Subhash Sharma

I earned my master’s degree in Physics in December of 2019 from Tribhuvan University, Nepal and, in the same year, I won one of the prestigious award ICPS WORLDWIDE GRANT from {iaps} which was given only two students around the world. Following the year, I was elected as editor of {jIAPS-2020}, it is a great privilege and an honour to have the experiences and opportunity that I do to meet brilliant people who share a great amplitude of spirit. I have presented my graduate research work in the couple of conferences and colloquia nationally/internationally. Primarily, my experimental research work was carried out in Plasma Physics Laboratory of Kathmandu University and additional laboratory ( IISC, IPR, CERN ) exposure have been propelled me to choose my academic career in experimental physics. Besides my academic degree, I aspire to build scientific think tank for the good of society, where guidance and leadership will be exercised as my skills, I love to being surrounded by brilliant people that share the vision of “science for good” and I’m truly humbled by their support. Meanwhile, I’m excited by the ever-growing challenges I’m faced with as my career unfolds, and keen to taking them on.
Farther from my degree, I also enjoy to enlighten the world’s fin-tech, blockchain and stocks. Entrepreneur and investment is also my cup of tea. I think physics is an excellent preparation and pretty good palace to the startup which lead you to an entrepreneur, if you want to see yourself a Physicist as well as an Entrepreneur.
